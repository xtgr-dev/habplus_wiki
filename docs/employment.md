# Employment

To get a job, visit either the Hospital or the BubbleJuice Bar.

For a job at the Hospital, step on the tiles near the stickies. Then type '3' for a job.
![Hospital Job](../static/img/Habbo_rIu2USz2OD.png)

For a job at the BubbleJuice Bar, step on the tiles opposite the till register. Then type '1' for a job.
![BubbleJuice Bar](../static/img/Habbo_46TUE8qGFt.png)
