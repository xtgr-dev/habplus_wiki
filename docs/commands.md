# Commands

## BASIC
- `:stats` - Shows the stats menu
- `:stats 1` - Shows your HP and energy
- `:stats 2` - Shows your gathering levels
- `:stats 3` - Shows combat stats
- `:stats 4` - Shows roleplay stats
- `:inv #` - Equips the item in the chosen inventory slot
- `:c or /c` - Turns combat mode on or off (if you're in passive mode, this will turn it off)
- `:discard (slot #)` or `/discard (slot #)` - Removes all items from a specific inventory slot
- `:bal`, `:balance`, `/bal`, `/balance` - Shows how much money you have (if you have an iPhone in your inventory, it also shows your bank balance)

---

## Hab+ MEDICAL
- `:offer` - Opens a menu to sell items.
- `:clear! / clear` - Revives a user after using defibrillators twice.
- `I Quit!` - Lets a user resign when standing on an "NPC Interactable" tile.

> **Note:** Double-clicking a user while on duty displays their current HP.

---

## BUBBLEJUICE CAFE
- `:offer` - Opens a menu to sell items.
- `I Quit! / I Quit!` - Lets a user resign when standing on an "NPC Interactable" tile.

> **Note:** Users can earn additional income by double-clicking spills that appear periodically in the cafe.
